# Summary

This is a improved design of the snap on sleeve/guide to prevent buckling of flexible filaments. It provides 360Ëšsupport around the filament & installs without disassembly or modification to the machine.  Illustration above shows how it snaps into the small gap between the extruder assembly and the start of the bowden tube & between the drive & pinch rollers.  

NOTES:
It is necessary to remove the (manually) modeled in support structure as shown in the above image. Obviously important to also clean any debris from the holes part surfaces & check for a smooth fit with 1.75mm filament before installation.  This printed very clean with little rework required using PLA.
